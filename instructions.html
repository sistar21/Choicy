Choicy Short Instructions

Instructions below contain some tips and tricks about how Choicy works.

Choose

Click "Choose" to make a new choice.

  • The choice will be made randomly.
  • The first line is for the title you choose and will not be considered.
  • Empty lines will be ignored.

Open Lists

Open.

  • Open a list to continue choose or make changes

Save

Click "Save" to save your the list.

  • Save your current list for later.
